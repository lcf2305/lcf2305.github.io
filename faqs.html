Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What type of ingredients can I blend in this blender?

A: Our blender is designed to handle a wide variety of ingredients, including fruits, vegetables, ice, nuts, seeds, and more. It's versatile enough to create smoothies, soups, sauces, dips, and even grind coffee beans or crush ice.

Q: How do I clean the blender after use?

A: Cleaning our blender is simple! Just disassemble the parts, wash the jar, lid, and blades with warm soapy water, and then rinse thoroughly. The blender jar is also dishwasher-safe for added convenience.

Q: Is the blender suitable for making nut butter or milling grains?

Absolutely! Our blender is powerful enough to create nut butter from various nuts and can mill grains into flour. The high-performance blades and motor allow for these versatile uses.

Q: What safety features does the blender have?

A: Safety is a priority. Our blender includes features such as overheat protection, automatic shut-off in case of overheating, and a secure locking mechanism to prevent the blender from operating unless properly assembled.

Q: Can I blend hot liquids in this blender?

A: Yes, our blender can handle blending hot liquids. However, it's essential to use caution and not fill the jar to the maximum capacity to prevent pressure build-up due to heat.

Q: How long is the warranty for this blender?

A: Our blender comes with a 2 Years warranty. Please refer to the product manual or contact our customer service for more details on warranty coverage and terms.

Q: What is the power consumption of the blender?

A: Our blender consumes 500 watts during operation, ensuring efficient performance while blending various ingredients.

Q: Can I purchase replacement parts for the blender?

A: Yes, we offer replacement parts for our blender. You can easily find and purchase them through our website or by contacting our customer support team.
