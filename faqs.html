Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What are the blender's specifications?

Answer 1: Our blender has a 1000W motor and comes with stainless steel blades.

Question 2: Is the blender dishwasher-safe?

Answer 2: Yes, all removable parts of our blender are dishwasher-safe.

Question 3: What is the warranty period for the blender?

Answer 3: Our blender comes with a one-year limited warranty.

Question 4: Can I blend hot liquids in the blender?

Answer 4: It's not recommended to blend hot liquids in our blender due to safety reasons.

Question 5: How can I clean the blender?

Answer 5: The blender can be cleaned by rinsing the jar and blades with warm, soapy water.
